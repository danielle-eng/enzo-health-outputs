## Formula Guide

### Running Hospitalization Rate
**Formula:** `(Sum of Hospitalizations to date) ÷ (Sum of Admissions to date) × 100`

**Example:** If 5 total admissions and 1 hospitalization by week 2:
1 ÷ 5 × 100 = **20.0%**

**CMS Benchmark:** 14.7% (national average)
**Sunrise Q1 Baseline:** 24.0%
**Q2 Target (PIP Interim):** ≤20.0%
**Q3 Goal (PIP Final):** ≤16.0%

---

### Running ED Utilization Rate
**Formula:** `(Sum of ED Visits to date) ÷ (Sum of Admissions to date) × 100`

**Example:** If 5 total admissions and 1 ED visit by week 2:
1 ÷ 5 × 100 = **20.0%**

**CMS Benchmark:** 8.2% (national average)
**Sunrise Q1 Baseline:** 10.0%
**Q2 Target:** ≤8.5%

---

### Timely Initiation Rate (SOC ≤14 days)
**Formula:** `(SOC Completions ≤14 days) ÷ (Admissions) × 100`

**Example:** If 5 total admissions and 4 SOCs completed by day 14:
4 ÷ 5 × 100 = **80.0%**

**CMS Benchmark:** 96.3%
**Sunrise Q1 Baseline:** 92.0%
**Q2 Target:** ≥96.0%

## Red Flag Escalation Protocol

**If any indicator turns RED (>2 pts above benchmark) in a single week, take the following steps immediately:**

1. **Within 24 hours:** Schedule emergency huddle (Clinical Director, Nursing Supervisor, QAPI)
2. **Huddle goals:**
   - Review all cases from the red-flag week (hospitalization or ED visits)
   - Identify root causes (symptom delay? staffing gap? patient non-compliance? clinical error?)
   - Determine if trend is temporary or systemic
3. **Root cause categories to assess:**
   - **Clinical:** Patient acuity surge, new diagnoses, seasonal spike
   - **Process:** Triage delays, visit scheduling issues, physician communication gaps
   - **Staff:** Sick leave, new staff, training gap, high-risk patient assignments
   - **External:** ED diversion, weather, hospital policy change, payer issue
4. **Action plan:** Document specific corrective actions and timeline
5. **Follow-up:** Re-assess weekly until rate returns to YELLOW or GREEN status
6. **Report:** Include red-flag event and corrective action in next monthly summary
