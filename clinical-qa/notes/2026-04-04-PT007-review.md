*Finding:* Peak flow documented as 210 L/min but note states "patient's personal best unknown; baseline assessment needed." This is not a critical deficiency but represents a missed opportunity for objective baseline comparison.

*Risk:* DOCUMENTATION QUALITY - LOW. This is a minor gap that does not affect overall ADR readiness.
*Suggestion:* Establish peak flow baseline at next visit if patient clinically stable. Note: "Peak flow 210 L/min; this is initial visit peak flow; recommend establishing patient's personal best over next 2-3 visits for trend comparison."
