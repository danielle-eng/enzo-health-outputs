## A Real Example: Before & After Documentation

**Before (PT003 note — DENIED by payer):**
*"Patient had wound dressing changed today. Wound measuring 2 cm x 2.5 cm. Patient tolerated well. Continue current plan."*

**After (our coaching):**
*"Skilled nursing is required for sterile assessment of wound healing and detection of infection signs. This post-ulcer wound (previously 3+ cm depth) requires skilled clinical judgment to prevent complications. Dressing change performed with sterile technique. Wound measured 2 cm x 2.5 cm (improved from 3 cm baseline). Minimal serous drainage, slight erythema consistent with normal healing phase."*

**Result:** Documentation approved. Claim paid. Staff trained for next 20 similar notes.
